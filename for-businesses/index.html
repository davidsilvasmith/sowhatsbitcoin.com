Bitcoin for Business

Bitcoin is the fastest most convenient way to get paid. It is also one of the fasteest and cheapest ways to receive U.S. dollars.

  • Safely accept payment from anyone in the world. (no chargebacks)
  • Integrations are much easier than any other type of payment.
  • Payments have no fees attached.
  • Bitpay eliminates volatility risk and lets you settle in cash.

Here are other ways bitcoin can help your business

  • Want some publicity? Unique, interesting, or surprising usage of bitcoin will get national attention.
  • Would micropayments provide new revenue opportunities? With bitcoin's tiny transaction fees sub dollar and even sub cent transactions are possible!
  • Would simplifying checkout increase conversions? With bitcoin customers don't need to create an account to pay you. Payments can be made by scanning a QR code.
  • Can't get a bank account?. No problem open a bitcoin wallet.
