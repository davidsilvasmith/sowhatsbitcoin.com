Bitcoin for Developers

Bitcoin is a computer science break through. Bitcoin's core innovation is a distributed ledger that is unforgeable. Basically it works like bittorrent but for sending and receiving payments.

Where to start?

  1. Satoshi's 8 page whitepaper will give a concise explanation of bitcoin.
  2. Buy $5 worth of bitcoin at Coinbase
  3. The company Blockchain.info has an easily consumable API to program against. Incidentally most consumer facing companies like coinbase, BitPay have APIs as well
